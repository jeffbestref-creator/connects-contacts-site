Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. We use cookies to enhance functionality, analyze usage, and remember preferences.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Provider
Essential Site navigation, form submissions Session connectscontacts.com

3. Managing Cookies

  • Browser Settings: Block/allow cookies via Chrome/Firefox/Safari settings.
  • Google Analytics Opt-out: Download opt-out browser add-on.
  • Do Not Track: Supported via browser preference.

4. Third Party Cookies

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous traffic data.
